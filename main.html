Summary

Inception is a science fiction movie that follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during dreams. He is given a chance to erase his criminal history by planting an idea into a target's mind.

Actors

The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, and Elliot Page as Ariadne.
